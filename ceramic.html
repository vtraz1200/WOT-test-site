Ceramic Packages

Platinum Package

  • 9+ Year Protective Coating

    Industry-leading ceramic technology that delivers extreme durability against environmental damage, oxidation, and UV rays.

  • Multi-Stage Paint Correction

    Multi-Stage Paint Correction Advanced machine polishing removes swirl marks, light scratches, and imperfections to restore a deep, mirror-like finish before the coating is applied.

  • Superior Hydrophobic Protection

    Water, dirt, and contaminants slide off effortlessly — making washing faster and keeping your vehicle cleaner longer.

  • Complete Surface Coverage

    Coating applied to paint, wheels, trim, and exposed surfaces for full-vehicle protection and a uniform finish.

Gold Package

  • 5–7 Year Durable Protection

    High-performance ceramic coating designed to shield your vehicle from UV rays, road grime, chemical stains, and environmental fallout.

  • Single-Step Paint Enhancement

    Removes the majority of swirl marks and light imperfections while significantly boosting gloss and clarity.

  • Deep Gloss Finish

    Enhances your vehicle’s color with a rich, wet-look shine that turns heads wherever you go.

  • Advanced Hydrophobic Technology

    Repels water, mud, and contaminants — making routine washes quicker and helping your vehicle stay cleaner between details.
